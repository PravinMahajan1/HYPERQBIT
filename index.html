Privacy Policy

DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖)

This Privacy Policy explains how DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖), a HYPERQB client app for AI news, podcasts, tutorials, article reading, and AI-assisted summaries, handles information.

Effective Date June 12, 2026
App Name DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖)
Brand HYPERQB

1. Plain-language summary

DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖) is designed to provide AI-related news, curated podcasts, video tutorials, article reading, and AI-assisted summaries.

We aim to collect only the minimum information needed to operate and improve the app. We do not sell users’ personal information.

The app may connect to backend services such as Supabase, Expo-related services, AI summary providers, external news sources, YouTube, podcast platforms, analytics tools, or crash-reporting tools depending on the final production configuration.

2. Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖) mobile application and related web pages operated under the HYPERQB brand.

It does not apply to external websites, news publishers, YouTube, podcast platforms, AI providers, or other third-party services that may be linked from the app. Those services have their own privacy policies.

3. Information we collect

3.1 Information you provide directly

The current version of DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖) does not require users to create an account, submit a profile, or provide personal identity information to access the main content.

If future versions add account features, contact forms, subscriptions, feedback forms, or community features, we may collect the information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, message content, or account preferences.

3.2 App usage and technical information

Depending on the final app configuration, we or our service providers may process limited technical and usage information, such as:

  • App version, device type, operating system, and general device diagnostics.
  • Crash logs, performance information, and error reports.
  • Content interaction data, such as opened articles, selected categories, viewed video listings, or podcast sections.
  • Approximate network information, such as IP address, automatically processed by hosting, database, security, or analytics providers.

3.3 Content and source data

The app may display AI news, summaries, podcast information, tutorial videos, source names, source URLs, publication dates, thumbnails, and related metadata.

Category Examples Purpose
Technical data App version, device type, crash logs Improve stability and fix bugs
Usage data Opened sections, selected categories, article interactions Improve content layout and app experience
Content data News titles, summaries, source links, podcast/video metadata Display AI-related information inside the app
AI processing data Article text or summaries sent for AI summary generation, if enabled Generate concise AI-assisted summaries

4. How we use information

We use information to operate, maintain, secure, and improve DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖). This may include using information to:

  • Load AI news, podcast, video, and tutorial content.
  • Provide category filters, article detail pages, and content navigation.
  • Support text-to-speech article reader functionality.
  • Generate or update AI-assisted summaries when the feature is used.
  • Monitor app performance, diagnose crashes, and fix technical issues.
  • Protect the app, backend, and users from misuse, abuse, or unauthorized access.
  • Maintain compliance with applicable platform, security, and legal requirements.

5. AI summary features

DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖) may include AI-assisted summary functionality. When this feature is used, article text, article metadata, or existing content summaries may be processed by an AI service to generate a concise summary.

AI summaries may contain mistakes. Verify important information from the original source.

AI-generated summaries are provided for informational and educational purposes only. They should not be treated as professional, legal, financial, medical, investment, or safety advice.

If a specific AI model or provider is mentioned in the app or store listing, that reference should accurately reflect the production configuration of the app.

6. Text-to-speech and audio reader

DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖) may provide a text-to-speech article reader that converts article text and summaries into audio playback.

This feature is used to read text aloud. The app does not use text-to-speech to record your voice.

Text-to-speech functionality may rely on device-level speech services or supported application libraries. Availability and behavior may vary by device, operating system, and language settings.

7. Third-party services

DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖) may use third-party services to provide backend storage, app functionality, analytics, crash reporting, content display, external links, or AI-assisted features.

Depending on the final production configuration, these services may include:

  • Supabase for backend database storage and content delivery.
  • Expo and related tooling for mobile application delivery and operation.
  • Google / Gemini-related services for AI-assisted summarization, if enabled.
  • YouTube or external video platforms when users open tutorial video content.
  • Podcast platforms or RSS sources when users open podcast content.
  • Analytics or crash-reporting providers if enabled to improve performance and reliability.

These third-party services may process information according to their own privacy policies, terms, and security practices.

8. External links, news sources, podcasts, and videos

The app may show summaries, titles, thumbnails, source names, publication dates, podcast metadata, video metadata, and links to external content.

When you tap an external link, you may leave the app and visit a third-party website or platform. We are not responsible for the content, policies, or privacy practices of external services.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any external website, publisher, video platform, podcast platform, or service you visit.

9. App permissions

DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖) is intended to request only permissions required for app functionality.

The app does not currently require access to sensitive permissions such as:

  • Precise location
  • Contacts
  • SMS or call logs
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Background location
  • Broad file storage access

If future features require additional permissions, the app should request them only when needed and explain why the permission is required.

10. Data retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to operate, maintain, secure, and improve the app, or as required by law.

Crash logs, analytics data, backend logs, and technical records may be retained for a limited period by us or our service providers to help diagnose issues and protect app security.

If future versions introduce user accounts, we will provide appropriate options for account deletion and data removal where required by applicable law and platform policy.

11. Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information associated with the app.

Security measures may include HTTPS connections, limited client-side access, environment variable controls, backend validation, database access rules, and restricted administrative credentials.

However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Children’s privacy

DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖) is not directed to children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to review and remove it where required.

13. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of personal information.

Since the current app does not require account creation for main content access, most users can stop using the app or uninstall it at any time.

If you contact us with a privacy request, we may need enough information to verify and process the request.

14. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect app updates, legal requirements, service provider changes, or operational improvements.

When we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
