Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how DAILY/AI (AILIVE🤖), operated under the HYPERQBIT brand, handles information when you use our mobile application.

DAILY/AI is an AI news, podcast, tutorial, and article reader client app. It provides curated AI updates, podcast listings, video tutorials, text-to-speech reading, and AI-assisted summaries.

Information We Collect

We designed DAILY/AI to collect as little personal information as possible. The app does not currently require users to create an account, submit a profile, or provide personal identity information to access the main content.

  • Technical Information: We may process app version, device type, operating system, crash logs, diagnostics, and performance data to keep the app stable and secure.
  • Usage Information: We may process basic app interactions such as opened articles, selected categories, podcast sections, video listings, or content screens.
  • Network Information: Backend, hosting, analytics, or security services may automatically process IP address and request data when the app connects to online services.

How We Use Information

We use information only to operate, maintain, improve, and secure the app. This includes using information to:

  • Provide AI news, podcasts, tutorial videos, and article content.
  • Load and synchronize content from backend services such as Supabase.
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Detect bugs, crashes, abuse, unauthorized access, or technical issues.
  • Generate or update AI-assisted summaries when the summary feature is used.

AI Summary Features

DAILY/AI may include AI-assisted summary functionality. When this feature is used, article text, article metadata, or existing content summaries may be processed by an AI service to generate a concise summary.

AI summaries may contain mistakes. Always verify important information from the original source.

AI-generated summaries are provided for informational and educational purposes only. They should not be treated as professional, legal, financial, medical, investment, or safety advice.

Text-to-Speech Article Reader

DAILY/AI may provide a text-to-speech feature that reads article text and summaries aloud. This feature is used to convert text into audio playback. The app does not use this feature to record your voice and does not require microphone access.

Third-Party Services

DAILY/AI may use third-party services to provide backend storage, app delivery, content delivery, analytics, crash reporting, AI summaries, external video links, and podcast links.

  • Supabase for backend database and content storage.
  • Expo for mobile app development and delivery services.
  • AI providers for AI-assisted summary generation, if enabled.
  • YouTube or external video platforms when opening tutorial video content.
  • Podcast platforms or RSS sources when opening podcast content.
  • Analytics or crash-reporting tools if enabled in production.

These third-party services may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

External Links and Sources

The app may display or link to external news sources, podcasts, videos, tutorials, websites, or other third-party content. We are not responsible for the content, accuracy, privacy practices, or policies of external services.

App Permissions

DAILY/AI only requests permissions needed for app functionality. The app does not currently require access to sensitive permissions such as precise location, contacts, SMS, call logs, camera, microphone, or broad file storage access.

Children’s Privacy

DAILY/AI is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Retention

We retain technical, usage, backend, crash, and diagnostic information only as long as reasonably necessary to operate, improve, secure, and maintain the app, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect app-related information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

User Choices

You may stop using the app or uninstall it at any time. If future versions introduce account creation, saved preferences, subscriptions, or user-generated content, we will provide appropriate access, correction, and deletion options where required by law and platform policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we update it, we will revise the “Last Updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
